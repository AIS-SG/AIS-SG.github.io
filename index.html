ABOUT ME

Standing at the intersection of Art and Engineering, I am a researcher dedicated to advancing Music AI. With a solid foundation in software engineering and extensive experience as a performing musician, I aim to apply Evolutionary Algorithms to enhance the creativity and accuracy of AI models.

My research career started with practical projects using Raspberry Pi and Chatbots, and has evolved into specialized academic research on Optical Music Recognition (OMR) and Light-weight Deep Learning, contributing to several publications.

Currently, I am seeking to overcome the structural limitations of probabilistic models (LLMs). My ultimate goal is to leverage Meta-Evolutionary Machine Intelligence to build an innovative system that not only generates music but understands and optimizes it like a human composer.
