ABOUT ME

I am a passionate individual who loves music and technology. I can play various instruments and have composed music. My journey with artificial intelligence began in high school, where I worked on projects like cell classification using a Raspberry Pi and developing a cafe kiosk chatbot.

As an undergraduate researcher, I have focused on lightweight handwriting recognition and optical music recognition (OMR), contributing to several publications. My goal is to combine my love for music and AI to create innovative solutions.
