ABOUT ME

I am an undergraduate researcher passionate about the intersection of Music and Artificial Intelligence. With a background in playing various instruments and composing, I aim to solve musical problems through technology.

My research journey began with practical projects using Raspberry Pi and Chatbots, and has evolved into academic research on Light-weight Deep Learning and OMR (Optical Music Recognition), contributing to several publications.

Currently, I am focusing on LLM Applications (Fine-tuning, RAG) and Music Information Retrieval (MIR). My ultimate goal is to develop an innovative Music Composition AI that bridges the gap between creative intuition and generative models.
